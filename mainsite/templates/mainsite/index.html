Property Maintenance

Resident Coordination Leave all your worries about scheduling tenants and technicians to our team of friendly dispatchers and project coordinators at RevCon. Our back-office specialists arrange the perfect in-house technician to arrive within the tenant’s most convenient time frame providing timely updates.


Additionally, we never disclose any information to the tenant other than scheduling and the general nature of repairs. Details such as prices and your future intentions are strictly confidential, moreover, if we hear any sort of legal action the tenant is pursuing, we will promptly diffuse the situation and inform you of the situation.


Our technicians always prioritize a way to skillfully repair the issue at hand before proceeding quoting for replacement. This is because we are keen on providing our clients with the most reliable and cost-effective solution possible.

About Us

RevCon is a construction management company. We manage construction projects from a simple remodel to large scale multi-family units. We provides full service rehabs, turns and maintenance, manages process on time and on budget.

RevCon has executed more than 300 projects in the last three years (and counting), totaling $50 million in assets.
In addition, we are also investors of our own properties and perform our own acquisitions.

RevCon has:
- Excellent management team
- General contractors and subcontractors all over the USA.

Our Clients

RevCon works with real estate investment and property management companies focused on renovating and leasing homes across the country.Our clients range from Real Estate investors to hedge funds. The vast majority of our customers operate on a large-scale covering most of the United States. They typically have assets in excess of $5 billion.
